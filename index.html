Drifty Car: Christmas Update (12/4/2023)

Drifty Car Christmas update comes out 12/5 around 11 am. New features include

  • Christmas Map
  • The Sleigh
  • Mars Removal

Christmas Map

The Christmas LTM(limited time mode) will be added for a 1 week. At the end of the week, the leaderboard holders will receive The Sleigh. The sleigh will not be available for purchase in the shop and will not be brought back again.

The Sleigh

The Sleigh can be aquired through placing top ten on the Christmas leaderboard by the end of the week. It will not return. It will feature medium speeds with strong handling similar to the ambulance.

Mars Removal

Unfortunately due to an attempt at storage saving, crucial parts of mars were deleted and the level(including asteroids level) will not return. The spaceship is currently unavailable for anyone who has not beaten mars. The spaceship may return in the future.

Future Christmas Updates

There will be Christmas updates each week for the next few weeks featuring new cars, levels, gamemodes, ect...
